Feedbex

Instagram Business Auto-Responder

What is Feedbex?

Feedbex is a tool that helps restaurants automatically respond to customer messages on Instagram.

How it works

  • The business connects its Instagram account using Instagram Login
  • Incoming messages are received via the Instagram API
  • The system generates automatic replies based on business information
  • Complex messages are forwarded to the business owner

Data usage

Feedbex only accesses messages and basic profile information after user authorization. No data is sold or used for advertising.
