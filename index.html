Feedbex

Instagram Business Auto-Responder for Restaurants

What is Feedbex?

Feedbex is a tool that helps restaurants automatically respond to customer messages on Instagram. Once connected, the system reads incoming DMs, generates relevant replies based on the restaurant's information (hours, menu, location), and sends them via the Instagram API. If a message requires human attention, it is forwarded to the business owner.

How it works

  • The restaurant owner connects their Instagram Business account using Instagram Login
  • Incoming customer messages are received via the Instagram Messaging API
  • The system generates automatic replies based on the restaurant's data
  • Complex or sensitive messages are forwarded to the business owner

Data usage

Feedbex only accesses messages and basic profile information after explicit user authorization. No data is sold or used for advertising.
