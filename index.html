FeedBack

Google Business Profile Reviews Assistant

What is FeedBack?

FeedBack is a lightweight assistant that helps businesses manage and draft responses to their Google Business Profile reviews.

How it works

  • FeedBack is used only by businesses that explicitly authorize access to their Google Business Profile.
  • The system drafts suggested replies to customer reviews.
  • Business owners always have full control over whether and how responses are published.

Data usage & compliance

  • FeedBack does not scrape public Google Maps data.
  • FeedBack accesses review data only for businesses that have granted permission.
  • No data is sold, shared, or used for advertising purposes.

Who is this for?

FeedBack is designed for small and medium-sized businesses that want to respond to customer reviews in a professional and timely manner.
