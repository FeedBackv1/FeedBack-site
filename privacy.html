Privacy Policy

FeedBack ("we", "our", or "the service") respects the privacy of businesses that use our service. This Privacy Policy explains how information is collected, used, and protected.

Information We Collect

FeedBack only processes information related to Google Business Profile reviews for businesses that have explicitly authorized access.

  • Review content (text and rating)
  • Business name and location identifier
  • Timestamps related to reviews

How We Use Information

Collected information is used solely to:

  • Draft suggested responses to customer reviews
  • Notify business owners about new reviews
  • Improve response quality and consistency

Data Access and Sharing

  • FeedBack does not scrape public Google Maps data
  • Data is accessed only for authorized businesses
  • No data is sold, shared, or used for advertising purposes

Data Storage and Retention

Data is stored only for as long as necessary to provide the service and may be deleted upon request by the business owner.

User Rights

Authorized businesses may request access, correction, or deletion of their data at any time.
