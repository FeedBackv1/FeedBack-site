Privacy Policy

Feedbex ("we", "our", or "the Service") is a business communication automation tool designed to help businesses respond to customer messages and reviews on platforms such as Instagram and Google Business Profile.

Information We Access

Instagram

After explicit authorization by the business owner via Instagram Login, Feedbex may access:

  • Instagram Business Account ID and basic profile information
  • Messages sent to the business via Instagram Direct Messages

Google Business Profile

After explicit authorization by the business owner via Google OAuth, Feedbex may access:

  • Google Business Profile account ID and basic business information
  • Customer reviews posted on Google (review text, star rating, reviewer name)

We only access the minimum data required to provide the service.

How We Use Information

  • Generate automated replies to customer messages on Instagram
  • Generate suggested replies to Google reviews for owner review and approval
  • Help businesses manage customer communication efficiently

Feedbex sends Instagram replies only as configured by the business. If a message requires human attention, it is forwarded to the business owner.

No reply is posted to Google Business Profile without explicit approval from the business owner.

Data Sharing

Feedbex does not sell, rent, or share user data with third parties. Data is not used for advertising or profiling.

Data Storage and Security

We store only necessary data such as access tokens and message logs required for system operation. Reasonable security measures are applied to protect this data.

Data Retention

Data is retained only as long as necessary to provide the service. If access is revoked, related data is deleted.

User Control

Businesses can revoke access at any time:

  • Instagram: through Instagram settings → Apps and Websites
  • Google: through Google Account settings → Security → Third-party apps with account access

Once access is revoked, Feedbex will stop accessing any new data.

Data Deletion

Users can request deletion of their data at any time.

  • By removing the app from their Instagram account
  • By sending a request to: idanschnap@gmail.com

Upon request, all associated data (including access tokens and stored messages) will be permanently deleted.

Compliance

Feedbex complies with Meta Platform Policies and only accesses Instagram data within the permissions granted by the user.

Feedbex complies with the Google API Services User Data Policy and only accesses Google data within the permissions granted by the user.

Contact

For any privacy-related inquiries:
Email: idanschnap@gmail.com
