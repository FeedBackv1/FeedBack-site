Privacy Policy

FeedBack ("we", "our", or "the Service") is a business communication automation platform designed to help businesses manage and respond to customer interactions across supported channels, including but not limited to Google Business Profile and social messaging platforms such as Instagram.

Information We Access

FeedBack accesses business-related data only after explicit authorization by the business owner. Depending on the connected platform, this may include:

  • Public customer reviews and ratings
  • Messages sent to a business account (e.g. Instagram Direct Messages)
  • Business profile metadata required to provide the service

How We Use Information

Data is used solely to:

  • Generate suggested responses to customer messages or reviews
  • Assist businesses in managing customer communications efficiently
  • Improve response quality and consistency

All publishing actions are controlled by the business. FeedBack does not post content on behalf of a business without user initiation or approval.

Data Sharing

FeedBack does not sell, rent, or share accessed data with third parties. Data is not used for advertising, profiling, or resale purposes.

Data Storage and Security

We apply reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect accessed data. Data is retained only for as long as necessary to provide the service or as required by law.

User Control

Businesses can revoke access at any time via the relevant platform (Google or Meta). Upon revocation, FeedBack will no longer access new data from that account.

Compliance

FeedBack complies with applicable Google Business Profile API Policies and Meta Platform Policies. We access platform data strictly within the permissions granted by the user.
